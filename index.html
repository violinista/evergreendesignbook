About the author

Milovan Jovicic

Product Designer · Strategist · Educator

Milovan Jovicic brings 25 years of shipping digital products — beginning as a formally trained programmer, building a decade of full-stack development experience, then evolving into a product designer and strategist trusted by some of the world's most demanding organisations.

His consulting work spans Apple, Amazon, UNICEF, Rakuten, Travelex, and Sizmek, alongside 50+ startups across sectors. Beyond client work, he founded the largest local UX design community, has organised more than 150 events since 2017, and teaches UX design at the Faculty of Technical Sciences in Novi Sad. He is a vocal advocate for ethics in design and believes thoughtful design can genuinely augment human potential.
